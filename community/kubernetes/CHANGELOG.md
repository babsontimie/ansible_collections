  - Initial migration of Kubernetes content from Ansible core (2.9 / devel), including content:
    - **Connection Plugins**:
      - `kubectl`
    - **Filter Plugins**:
      - `k8s_config_resource_name`
    - **Inventory Source**:

      - `openshift`
    - **Lookup Plugins**:
      - `k8s`
    - **Modules**:
      - `k8s`
      - `k8s_auth`
      - `k8s_info`
      - `k8s_scale`
      - `k8s_service`
